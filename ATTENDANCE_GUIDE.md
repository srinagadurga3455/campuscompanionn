# Attendance System - Quick Start Guide

## For Faculty Members

### How to Mark Attendance

1. **Login** to your faculty account
2. From the dashboard, click **"ðŸ“‹ Mark Attendance"** button
3. **Select Class Details**:
   - Choose Branch (e.g., Computer Science)
   - Select Year (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th)
   - Enter Section (optional, e.g., A, B)
   - Enter Subject name (required, e.g., "Data Structures")
   - Select Date (defaults to today)
4. Click **"Load Students"** button
5. **Mark Attendance**:
   - Each student row shows their current attendance percentage
   - Click **"Present"** (green) or **"Absent"** (red) for each student
   - Default is Present for all students
6. Click **"Save Attendance"** to submit

### Tips for Faculty
- You can update attendance for past dates
- Attendance percentage updates automatically after saving
- Students with <75% attendance are highlighted in red
- You can change attendance for a date by loading it again and re-saving

## For Students

### How to View Your Attendance

1. **Login** to your student account
2. Your **attendance percentage** appears on the dashboard
3. Click on the **attendance card** to see detailed view
4. **Detailed View Shows**:
   - Overall attendance percentage
   - Total classes attended vs missed
   - Subject-wise breakdown with percentages
   - Recent attendance records with dates

## Troubleshooting

### Faculty Issues

**Q: Students not loading?**
- Ensure you've selected both Branch and Year
- Check that students are enrolled in your class
- Verify you have the correct permissions

**Q: Can't save attendance?**
- Make sure Subject field is filled
- Check that you've marked attendance for at least one student
- Verify your internet connection

**Q: Need to update past attendance?**
- Simply select the past date
- Load students
- Update the attendance
- Save again

### Student Issues

**Q: Attendance not showing?**
- Check if your faculty has marked attendance
- Refresh the page
- Ensure you're logged in

**Q: Percentage seems wrong?**
- Percentage is calculated as: (Present Classes / Total Classes) Ã— 100
- Check subject-wise breakdown for details
- Contact your faculty if you notice discrepancies

## Best Practices

### For Faculty
1. Mark attendance daily for accuracy
2. Use the same subject name consistently (e.g., always "Data Structures" not "DS")
3. Review the summary cards before saving
4. Keep records up to date

### For Students
1. Check your attendance regularly
2. Monitor subject-wise percentages
3. Maintain at least 75% attendance
4. Contact faculty immediately if you notice errors
